Welcome to my website

About me:

Hello, and thank you for coming to my website. I am a pianist, organist, and composer who lives in Grand Junction Colorado. I play in Swing City Express, the Western Colorado Jazz Orchestra, the Clark Gault Dectet, and many other bands. I also play for various church and school organizations, including D51, First United Methodist, and First Congregational United Church of Christ. Feel free to click around, and see what I have going on!

Hear my playing and my compositions.

  • Invention in the Style of Bach.. I have always enjoyed counterpoint, but this was my first hack at working some out myself! I had spent the summer of 2021 studying from Gradus ad Parnassum, but this was my first time writing a proper piece in mostly invertable counterpoint without any kind of cantus firmus. I hope you enjoy this recording from my most recent recital!
  • Maurice Ravel, Jeux d'eux. I love this piece of music, and I think this is a good example of where I am right now in my piano playing. I still have a lot to work on, but I feel that I have made some very good progress.
